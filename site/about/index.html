THE STORY

Bilingual by default. Detail-obsessed by design.

I'm Sarah — most people call me Sarah Dee. I'm a licensed REALTOR® at Keller Williams Village Square in Ridgewood, and the founder of The SD Group.

I started this group because I'd watched enough friends and family navigate a $1M-plus home purchase with an agent who didn't fully see them — who didn't speak their parents' language, who treated the biggest financial decision of their life like a template transaction.

The SD Group is the alternative. Every home search starts with the same question I'd ask a family member: What is the life you're trying to build, and which home serves it? From there we work through the systems — financing, inspection, negotiation, closing — with the full support of the largest real estate brokerage in the world behind us, and the quiet discipline of a team that treats every transaction as though our name is on the deed too.

Bilingual by default. Detail-obsessed by design. Here for the long relationship, not the single closing.
