ABOUT · SARAH DEE

Licensed in New Jersey. Rooted in Ridgewood. Fluent in both languages your family speaks.

I'm Sarah — most people call me Sarah Dee. I'm a licensed REALTOR® at Keller Williams Village Square in Ridgewood, and the founder of The SD Group.

I started this group because I'd watched enough friends and family navigate a $1M-plus home purchase with an agent who didn't fully see them — who didn't speak their parents' language, who treated the biggest financial decision of their life like a template transaction.

The SD Group is the alternative. Every home search starts with the same question I'd ask a family member: What is the life you're trying to build, and which home serves it? From there we work through the systems — financing, inspection, negotiation, closing — with the full support of the largest real estate brokerage in the world behind us, and the quiet discipline of a team that treats every transaction as though our name is on the deed too.

Bilingual by default. Detail-obsessed by design. Here for the long relationship, not the single closing.

The SD Method.

Twelve steps, from first conversation to the long relationship. The same preparation every time — because a home is the biggest financial decision most families will ever make.

STEP
01/ 12
The First Conversation
  1. 01 · The First Conversation

    The First Conversation.

    No script. We talk about what you actually want — the life behind the house.

  2. 02

    Financial Groundwork.

    Mortgage pre-approval, closing-cost planning, and the honest math of what's yours to work with.

  3. 03

    The Map.

    Which Bergen County towns match your life — commute, schools, community, quiet.

  4. 04

    The Search.

    Curated listings filtered by everything that matters, including the quiet stuff no MLS field captures.

  5. 05

    Touring with Intent.

    Every home visit is a decision-making session, not a tour.

  6. 06

    The Strategy.

    Before we offer, we study comps, days-on-market, seller motivation, and the micro-season of the specific street.

  7. 07

    The Offer.

    Written to win without overpaying — and in both languages if your co-signer needs it.

  8. 08

    The Negotiation.

    Every counter is an opportunity. We protect your leverage and your peace.

  9. 09

    Inspection & Due Diligence.

    We read every report, flag every risk, and decide together what's negotiable.

  10. 10

    The Closing Countdown.

    Attorney, title, lender, appraisal — orchestrated so you never chase a signature.

  11. 11

    Closing Day.

    We walk through the home once more. Then we hand you the keys.

  12. 12

    The Long Relationship.

    A year later I still remember your anniversary closing date. Because this wasn't just a transaction.
