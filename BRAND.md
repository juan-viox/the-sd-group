## ABOUT — Sarah Dee

### English
**Headline:** Licensed in New Jersey. Rooted in Ridgewood. Fluent in both languages your family speaks.

**Body:**
I'm Sarah — most people call me Sarah Dee. I'm a licensed REALTOR® at Keller Williams Village Square in Ridgewood, and the founder of The SD Group.

I started this group because I'd watched enough friends and family navigate a $1M-plus home purchase with an agent who didn't fully see them — who didn't speak their parents' language, who treated the biggest financial decision of their life like a template transaction.

The SD Group is the alternative. Every home search starts with the same question I'd ask a family member: *What is the life you're trying to build, and which home serves it?* From there we work through the systems — financing, inspection, negotiation, closing — with the full support of the largest real estate brokerage in the world behind us, and the quiet discipline of a team that treats every transaction as though our name is on the deed too.

Bilingual by default. Detail-obsessed by design. Here for the long relationship, not the single closing.

## THE SD METHOD — Sticky Stack

### English — 12 Steps
1. **The First Conversation.** No script. We talk about what you actually want — the life behind the house.
2. **Financial Groundwork.** Mortgage pre-approval, closing-cost planning, and the honest math of what's yours to work with.
3. **The Map.** Which Bergen County towns match your life — commute, schools, community, quiet.
4. **The Search.** Curated listings filtered by everything that matters, including the quiet stuff no MLS field captures.
5. **Touring with Intent.** Every home visit is a decision-making session, not a tour.
6. **The Strategy.** Before we offer, we study comps, days-on-market, seller motivation, and the micro-season of the specific street.
7. **The Offer.** Written to win without overpaying — and in both languages if your co-signer needs it.
8. **The Negotiation.** Every counter is an opportunity. We protect your leverage and your peace.
9. **Inspection & Due Diligence.** We read every report, flag every risk, and decide together what's negotiable.
10. **The Closing Countdown.** Attorney, title, lender, appraisal — orchestrated so you never chase a signature.
11. **Closing Day.** We walk through the home once more. Then we hand you the keys.
12. **The Long Relationship.** A year later I still remember your anniversary closing date. Because this wasn't just a transaction.

### Español — 12 Pasos
1. **La Primera Conversación.** Sin libreto. Hablamos de lo que realmente quieres — la vida detrás de la casa.
2. **Base Financiera.** Pre-aprobación de hipoteca, planificación de costos de cierre, y las cuentas honestas de con qué estás trabajando.
3. **El Mapa.** Qué pueblos de Bergen County encajan con tu vida — transporte, escuelas, comunidad, tranquilidad.
4. **La Búsqueda.** Listados seleccionados filtrados por todo lo que importa, incluyendo lo silencioso que ningún MLS captura.
5. **Visitar con Propósito.** Cada visita es una sesión de decisión, no un tour.
6. **La Estrategia.** Antes de ofrecer, estudiamos comparables, días en el mercado, motivación del vendedor, y la micro-temporada de la calle específica.
7. **La Oferta.** Escrita para ganar sin sobrepagar — y en ambos idiomas si tu co-firmante lo necesita.
8. **La Negociación.** Cada contra-oferta es una oportunidad. Protegemos tu posición y tu paz.
9. **Inspección y Diligencia.** Leemos cada reporte, marcamos cada riesgo, y decidimos juntos qué se negocia.
10. **La Cuenta Regresiva al Cierre.** Abogado, título, prestamista, tasación — orquestados para que nunca persigas una firma.
11. **El Día del Cierre.** Caminamos la casa una vez más. Luego te entregamos las llaves.
12. **La Relación Larga.** Un año después todavía recuerdo tu aniversario de cierre. Porque no fue solo una transacción.
